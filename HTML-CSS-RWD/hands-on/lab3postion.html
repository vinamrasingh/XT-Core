Come see the fantastic Coit Tower!

Did you know that Coit Tower is a monument to the firefighters of San Francisco? It rises 210 feet over telegraph hill and provides stunning views of the city.

Money for the tower was provided by Lillie Coit , an eccentric socialite who had deep connections to the city's firefighters.

If you want to visit, consider walking! Parking is limited at the tower, and it is often faster (and more scenic) to just walk.
